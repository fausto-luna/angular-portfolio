LUZ PRIMERA

LUZ PRIMERA

Series of 35mm film photographs

2008 - 2011

"LUZ PRIMERA" (ENG: "First Light" ) is an intimate approach to my own life driven by the feeling of melancholy. During this period of time, photography played the role of survival mechanism, the reflex act of clinging to the ephemeral matter of an instant.

This work is the proof of the birth of my photographic gaze, the first light that breaks the darkness of unconsciousness. A collection of memories caused by the sweet pain I feel when watching my time slip like sand through my fingers.
