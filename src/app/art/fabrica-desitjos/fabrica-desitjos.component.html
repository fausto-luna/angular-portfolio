LA TÈRMICA - FABRICA DE DESITJOS

La Tèrmica - Fàbrica de Desitjos

Performance, live-cinema, acting, photography, installation.

2019

Family-oriented show and workshop performed in collaboration with artists Clara Sáez and Maria Jover at La Tèrmica de Roca Umbert in Granollers, Barcelona during Christmas 2019.

Transformation of an old thermal power plant into a wish factory where families collectively manufactured individual and global wishes. Before manufacturing their wishes, children and adults received a little training through sounds and a live-cinema performance. Later they began the elaboration of the piece at the assembly area, passing through the light-fixation boot , finishing the process with the placement of the wish in the storage area, obtaining as a result an installation of collective creation.

Commissioned by Roca Umbert Fàbrica De Les Arts
