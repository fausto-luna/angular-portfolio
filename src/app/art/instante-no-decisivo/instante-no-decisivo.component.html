INSTANTE NO DECISIVO

INSTANTE NO DECISIVO

Series of photographs

2012 - 2019

Non-decisive moment: an instant that is not relevant. A moment that get lost on the way to History. Piece of existence that is ignored, containing things left unsaid. What does not prevail and dies in the shadow of what is widely considered as important.

“INSTANTE NO DECISIVO” (ENG: “Non-decisive instant”) is a project of presence, proximity and resistance. An exercise of exploration and documentation of the untold History.
