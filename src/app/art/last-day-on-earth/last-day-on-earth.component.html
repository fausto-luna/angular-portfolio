LAST DAY ON EARTH

LAST DAY ON EARTH

Photographs, videos and texts.

2017-2020

What would you do in your last day on earth? This project was born out of this question. What would you do? How would you spend your last day on this planet? Contemplation. I personally want to contemplate reality, nature, and peacefully feel all that beauty.

“LAST DAY ON EARTH” is this imaginary space where I contemplate what is around me with no other intention than trying to connect with the Now. To be present. Here and now all the beauty is part of me, and I am part of everything.

Work still in progress.
