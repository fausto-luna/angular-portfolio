Rating

How much do you like ice cream?

Thanks to this app you can easily find out how serious is ice cream for you. For me? Very! I am an ice cream freak, that is why I wanted to help everybody to know, to think about this.

With this rating system we can mesure in a scale 1 to 5 the importance of ice cream in your life. Only 3 steps;

  • 1 - Hover the mouse pointer over the numbers -tap if you browse on mobile- to ask yourself

  • 2 - Hover the mouse pointer over the ice cream icons to get some feedback before the decision making

  • 3 - Rate by clicking -or taping- the icon you think is more appropriate.

HTML5 + CSS3 + JavaScript

This approach to a rating system could be implemented in any application, or e-commerce website in order to get feedback of products or services from users.

It was built with plain HTML, CSS and JavaScript as a result of an exercise for the Front-end training program I have enrolled in at IT Academy of Barcelona Activa.
