Rocket controller

Let the race begin

This app gets you closer to your dream of being an astronaut and pilot your own rocket. How about that?

First you should create your rocket. Once your brand new rocket is created it will be identified by an 8-character code and a number of thrusters will be assigned. Also, you can create two rockets and organise a rocket race. Rocket 1 will always have 3 thrusters and Rocket 2 will have 6 thrusters.

Now you are ready to launch your spacecraft! Accelerate and decelerate by pressing the corresponding as. Be aware that the maximum speed of your rocket is limited by the power of its thrusters.

Rocket 1: 3 thrusters with a maximum power of 10, 30 and 80.

Rocket 2: 6 thrusters with a maximum power of 30, 40, 50, 50, 30 and 10.

HTML5 + CSS3 + TypeScript

This approach is the result of an object-oriented programming (OOP) exercise built with HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap and TypeScript (ES6) for the Front End Programme @ IT Academy [Cybernarium | Barcelona Activa].

The goal of this project is to implement the Model View Controller (MVC) design pattern in order to distribute the application's code in their own containers. A neat and clean organisation of the components that will make life easier. Also, dealing with variables, conditionals, loops and arrays was a point of building this little app.
